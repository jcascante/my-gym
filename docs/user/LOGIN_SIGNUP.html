🔐 Login & Sign-Up

Get started with MyGym and manage your account securely

Overview

MyGym is a personalized workout program manager that creates custom workout programs tailored to your fitness goals, experience level, and available time. To use MyGym, you need to create an account with a unique email address and password. This guide walks you through creating your account, logging in, and managing your session securely.

Before You Start

  • Email Address: You'll need a valid email address. This is how you log in to MyGym
  • Password: Create a strong password that you haven't used elsewhere
  • Browser: Have a modern web browser open (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

How to Create an Account

Creating a new MyGym account takes just a few minutes.

Step 1: Go to the Sign-Up Page

  1. Open the MyGym application
  2. Click "Sign Up" if you see the login page

Step 2: Enter Your Email

  • Type an email address you have access to
  • This email will be how you log in from now on
  • Make sure you've typed it correctly

Step 3: Create Your Password

  • Type a password (at least 8 characters)
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols (like !@#) for better security
  • Confirm your password by typing it again
Passwords are case-sensitive, so remember how you typed it

Step 4: Add Your Name

  • First Name: Your first name (required)
  • Last Name: Your last name (optional but helpful)
  • These will be used to personalize your experience

Step 5: Complete Sign-Up

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button
  2. You're automatically logged in and sent to set up your profile
  3. Continue through the onboarding to tell us about your fitness goals

✅ You're done! Your account is created and you're ready to start using MyGym.

How to Log In

Ready to start a workout? Log in to your account anytime.

Step 1: Go to the Login Page

  • Open the MyGym application
  • You'll see the login page if you're not already logged in

Step 2: Enter Your Email and Password

  • Email: Type the email address you used when creating your account
  • Password: Type your password
  • Double-check for typos—passwords are case-sensitive

Step 3: Click "Log In"

  • If your details are correct, you'll be logged in
  • You'll see your dashboard and can start using MyGym

How to Log Out

Always log out when you're done, especially on shared devices.

Step 1: Find the Logout Button

  • Look in the top-right corner of your dashboard
  • You'll see a "Logout" button

Step 2: Click Logout

  • Your session ends immediately
  • You're returned to the login page
  • Your account is secure

Troubleshooting

❓ I forgot my password

Solution: Contact MyGym support with the email from your account. We'll help you reset your password and regain access.

❓ I can't log in with my email

Solution:

  1. Check you're using the exact email from sign-up (uppercase/lowercase matters)
  2. Make sure Caps Lock is off on your keyboard
  3. Verify you typed the password correctly
  4. Try resetting your password (contact support)
❓ I don't recognize my email anymore

Solution: If you signed up with an old email you no longer use, contact support. We can help verify your identity and update your account.

❓ The login button doesn't work

Solution:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try refreshing the page (press F5 or Cmd+R)
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
❓ My session keeps timing out

Solution: You're logged out after a period of inactivity for security. Just log back in—your data is safe. If you'll be away, log out manually instead.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid birthdays or common words
  • Consider using a password manager to securely store your MyGym password
  • Make sure you remember which email you signed up with
  • Always log out on shared computers or phones
  • Check your email for any MyGym account notifications

Related Features

After you've logged in, you might want to:

  • Set up your fitness profile — Tell MyGym about your goals and experience
  • Generate your first workout program — Get a personalized routine
  • Start tracking workouts — Log your daily exercises

Security Notes

  • Your password is encrypted when transmitted and stored securely on our servers
  • We never share your account information with third parties
  • Log out on shared devices to protect your account
  • If you suspect suspicious activity, change your password immediately
Contact MyGym support if you have any questions about logging in or signing up.
