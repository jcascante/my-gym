🎯 Getting Started with MyGym

Complete your profile on first login and start training

What Happens When You First Join

MyGym is personalized to your fitness goals. When you sign up and log in for the first time, we'll ask you a few quick questions about your fitness experience and availability. This helps us create the perfect workout program just for you.

✓ First-Time Experience Your profile setup takes just 2-3 minutes. Answer a few questions about your goals and experience, and we'll build your personalized workout program.

Your First Login: Step-by-Step

  1. Sign Up or Log In — Enter your email and password. If you're returning, you'll go straight to your dashboard.
  2. Complete Your Profile — First-time users see a profile form asking:
    • Fitness Goal: What are you training for? (Build muscle, lose weight, increase endurance, etc.)
    • Experience Level: Are you a beginner, intermediate, or advanced athlete?
    • Time Available: How many hours per week can you train?
  3. Click "Create Profile" — Your profile is saved to our servers
  4. Welcome to MyGym! — You're taken to your personalized dashboard

Important: What Happens to Returning Users

Already have an account? You won't see the profile form again. When you log in:

  • We remember your existing profile
  • You skip the setup and go straight to your dashboard
  • If you want to change your profile later, you can do that from your Settings menu
💡 Pro Tip: Your profile isn't locked in. You can change your fitness goal, experience level, or availability anytime from your account settings. MyGym will adjust your workout program accordingly.

Profile Questions Explained

🎯 Fitness Goal

What's your primary reason for training? Examples:

  • Build Muscle — Increase strength and size
  • Lose Weight — Reduce body fat and lean down
  • Increase Endurance — Build cardiovascular fitness
  • General Fitness — Balanced health and wellness
  • Athletic Performance — Train for a sport or event

📊 Experience Level

How long have you been training regularly?

  • Beginner — Less than 1 year of regular training
  • Intermediate — 1-3 years of consistent training
  • Advanced — 3+ years, comfortable with complex programs

⏰ Hours Per Week

How many hours can you realistically dedicate to training each week?

  • 3 hours: 3-4 workouts per week
  • 5 hours: 4-5 workouts per week
  • 7+ hours: 5-6 workouts per week

Be honest! It's better to underestimate than overcommit. You can always increase later.

What Happens After You Create Your Profile

✓ Your workout program is generated — Based on your goal, experience, and availability

MyGym creates a personalized program with:

  • Recommended exercises for your goal
  • Sets, reps, and weight guidance
  • Rest days scheduled appropriately
  • Progressive difficulty as you get stronger

Troubleshooting

❓ I'm a returning user but don't see my program

If you've logged in before but your profile isn't showing, try: 1) Refresh the page, 2) Clear your browser cache, 3) Log out and log back in. If the issue persists, contact support.

❓ Can I skip the profile setup?

No, we need your profile to create a program. But it only takes 2-3 minutes, and you can always change it later from Settings.

❓ What if I don't know my fitness level?

Go with your gut. Beginner: Never trained before or inconsistent. Intermediate: Train regularly (1-3 years). Advanced: Train seriously (3+ years). You can update this anytime.

❓ I entered the wrong hours per week. Can I change it?

Yes! Go to Settings → Edit Profile and update your availability. Your program will adjust automatically.

❓ Why is my profile required?

Your profile tells us your fitness goal, experience, and time availability. Without this, we can't create a safe, effective, personalized program. That's the whole point of MyGym!
