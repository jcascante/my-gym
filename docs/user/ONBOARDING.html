🎯 Getting Started with MyGym

Complete your profile on first login and start training

What Happens When You First Join

MyGym is personalized to your fitness goals. When you sign up and log in for the first time, we'll walk you through a short step-by-step wizard about your body, experience, and schedule. This helps us create the perfect workout program just for you.

✓ First-Time Experience Your profile setup takes just 2-3 minutes across 5 short steps. Answer a few questions about your body, experience, and goals, and we'll build your personalized workout program.

The Onboarding Wizard

Instead of one long scrolling form, your profile is broken into 5 bite-sized steps. A progress bar at the top always shows exactly where you are:

  1. Personal Information — Age, gender, weight, and height
  2. Fitness Level — Your experience level and how active you currently are
  3. Workout Preferences — Your training focus, days per week, session length, and available equipment
  4. Your Goals — Optional short-term and medium-term goals in your own words
  5. Additional Information — Optional notes on injuries or physical limitations

On each step, click Next to continue. If a required field on that step is empty, you'll see a reminder message and stay on the same step until it's filled in — so you always know exactly what's missing. Changed your mind about something on an earlier step? Click Back to revisit it; your answers stay filled in. On the last step, the Next button becomes Complete Onboarding.

💡 Tip: The Goals and Additional Information steps are optional — feel free to click Next through them if you'd rather fill them in later from Settings.

Your First Login: Step-by-Step

  1. Sign Up or Log In — Enter your email and password. If you're returning, you'll go straight to your dashboard.
  2. Complete Your Profile — First-time users see the 5-step onboarding wizard described above, one short screen at a time
  3. Click "Complete Onboarding" — Shown on the final step; your profile is saved to our servers
  4. Welcome to MyGym! — You're taken to your personalized dashboard

Important: What Happens to Returning Users

Already have an account? You won't see the profile form again. When you log in:

  • We remember your existing profile
  • You skip the setup and go straight to your dashboard
  • If you want to change your profile later, you can do that from your Settings menu
💡 Pro Tip: Your profile isn't locked in. You can change your fitness goal, experience level, or availability anytime from your account settings. MyGym will adjust your workout program accordingly.

Wizard Steps Explained

1️⃣ Personal Information

Your basic body stats, used to size your program correctly:

  • Age, Gender, Weight (kg), and Height (cm) — all required

2️⃣ Fitness Level

How trained and how active you currently are:

  • Experience Level — Beginner (less than 1 year training), Intermediate (1-3 years), or Advanced (3+ years)
  • Activity Level — Sedentary, Lightly Active, Moderately Active, or Very Active, describing your day-to-day movement outside the gym

3️⃣ Workout Preferences

What your training should focus on and how it fits your schedule:

  • Fitness Focus — Strength, Endurance, Flexibility, Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, or General Fitness
  • Days per Week — How many days you can train (1-7)
  • Workout Duration — How long each session should run, in minutes
  • Equipment Access — Home, Bodyweight Only, or full Gym access

Be honest about your availability! It's better to underestimate days and duration than overcommit. You can always increase later.

4️⃣ Your Goals (optional)

Free-text fields to describe, in your own words, what you're working toward — a short-term goal (e.g. "build a consistent habit") and a medium-term goal (e.g. "gain muscle and improve endurance"). Skip these if you're not sure yet.

5️⃣ Additional Information (optional)

A free-text box for any injuries or physical limitations we should account for when building your program.

What Happens After You Create Your Profile

✓ Your workout program is generated — Based on your goal, experience, and availability

MyGym creates a personalized program with:

  • Recommended exercises for your goal
  • Sets, reps, and weight guidance
  • Rest days scheduled appropriately
  • Progressive difficulty as you get stronger

Troubleshooting

❓ I'm a returning user but don't see my program

If you've logged in before but your profile isn't showing, try: 1) Refresh the page, 2) Clear your browser cache, 3) Log out and log back in. If the issue persists, contact support.

❓ Can I skip the profile setup?

The required fields on each step (Personal Information, Fitness Level, Workout Preferences) must be filled in before you can click Next — we need those to create a safe program. The Goals and Additional Information steps are optional, so you can click through them. The whole wizard takes 2-3 minutes, and you can always change your answers later from Settings.

❓ What if I don't know my fitness level?

Go with your gut. Beginner: Never trained before or inconsistent. Intermediate: Train regularly (1-3 years). Advanced: Train seriously (3+ years). You can update this anytime.

❓ I clicked Next but nothing happened

A required field on that step is probably still empty — you'll see a message asking you to fill in all required fields before continuing. Scroll up to check for any blank dropdown or number field on the current step.

❓ I entered the wrong days per week or duration. Can I change it?

Yes! While onboarding, click Back to return to the Workout Preferences step and edit it — your other answers stay filled in. After onboarding, go to Settings → Edit Profile and update your availability. Your program will adjust automatically.

❓ Why is my profile required?

Your profile tells us your fitness goal, experience, and time availability. Without this, we can't create a safe, effective, personalized program. That's the whole point of MyGym!

Tips for Success

✓ Be honest about your experience — A program that's too hard leads to burnout. You can always level up.
✓ Underestimate your time — It's better to say 3 hours and train 4 than promise 7 and train 2.
✓ Save your profile updates — Click "Save Changes" to ensure your updates are stored.
✓ Don't worry about perfection — You can change everything anytime. Get started now.
