# Login & Sign-Up Guide

## About MyGym

MyGym is a personalized workout program manager that creates custom workout programs tailored to your fitness goals, experience level, and available time. Track your daily workouts, log performance metrics, and get adaptive feedback to optimize your training journey.

## Getting Started

To start using MyGym, you'll need to create an account. Follow the steps below to sign up and log in.

### Creating a New Account

1. **Navigate to the Sign-Up Page**
   - Visit the MyGym application at the provided URL
   - Click on "Sign Up" if you don't already have an account

2. **Enter Your Email Address**
   - Provide a valid email address
   - This email will be used to log in to your account
   - Ensure the email is something you have access to

3. **Create a Strong Password**
   - Password must be at least 8 characters long
   - Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters for security
   - Confirm your password by re-entering it
   - **Tip**: Use a unique password that you don't use elsewhere

4. **Enter Your Name**
   - First Name: Your first name
   - Last Name: Your last name (optional but recommended)
   - These will be used to personalize your experience

5. **Complete the Sign-Up**
   - Click the "Sign Up" button
   - You'll be automatically logged in and directed to the onboarding flow
   - Continue with onboarding to set up your fitness profile

### Logging In

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**
   - Visit the MyGym application
   - You'll be directed to the login page if you're not already authenticated

2. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - **Email**: The email address you used when signing up
   - **Password**: The password you created during sign-up

3. **Click "Log In"**
   - If your credentials are correct, you'll be logged in
   - You'll be directed to your dashboard

4. **Troubleshooting Login Issues**
   - **Forgot your password?** Contact support to reset your password
   - **Can't log in?** Verify your email and password are correct
   - **Email not recognized?** Make sure you're using the exact email from sign-up

### Logging Out

1. **From the Dashboard**
   - Look for the "Logout" button (typically in the top-right corner)
   - Click it to log out of your account
   - You'll be directed back to the login page

2. **Session Security**
   - Your session is secure and expires after a period of inactivity
   - Always log out when using a shared device
   - Your login tokens are stored securely in HTTP-only cookies

### Password Security

- **Never share your password** with anyone, including support staff
- **Change your password regularly** for better security
- **Use a unique password** that you don't use for other accounts
- If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact support immediately

### Account Recovery

If you lose access to your account:
1. Contact MyGym support with the email associated with your account
2. We'll verify your identity and help you regain access
3. Consider enabling additional security measures if available

### Privacy & Security

- Your login credentials are encrypted and transmitted securely
- Your password is hashed using industry-standard bcrypt
- Your account data is protected and not shared with third parties
- Review our privacy policy for more information

## Next Steps

Once you've successfully logged in:
1. Complete your fitness profile in the onboarding flow
2. Get a personalized workout program generated for you
3. Start tracking your daily workouts
4. Monitor your progress with our analytics tools
