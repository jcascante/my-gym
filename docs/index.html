Welcome to MyGym

MyGym is your personalized workout program manager. Complete your onboarding profile, receive an AI-generated workout program tailored to your goals and fitness level, and track your daily workouts with adaptive feedback to optimize your training journey.

Features

🔐 Account Management

Create your MyGym account, log in securely, and manage your authentication with our secure JWT-based system.

👤 Profile Setup

🎯 Workout Programs

Receive AI-generated workout programs customized to your fitness goals and experience level. Coming Soon

📝 Daily Tracking
